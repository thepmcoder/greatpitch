# Test Scenario P06: Elevate

## Product Brief

**Product:** Elevate — AI-powered student career and research navigation platform
**Creators:** Krishna Prabu and Tanmai Raghava (college project)
**Stage:** Working prototype (mobile + web app)
**Tagline:** Your Choices • Your Preparation • Your Confidence • Your Success

**The Problem:**
Students are overwhelmed navigating career and research paths. They're scattered across dozens of platforms — LinkedIn, Unstop, Internshala, college portals — trying to answer basic questions: What should I prepare for internships? Which professors are open to interns? What should I focus on? How do I keep track of hackathons, contests, and deadlines?

The data from a student survey is stark:
- Over 80% feel least informed about how to prepare for various roles and understanding company shortlisting patterns
- Over 80% feel they need the most direction in building a strong profile
- Over 60% need clearer guidance on company expectations, preparation resources, and where to begin
- Only 14% are satisfied with the current effort and process of finding information
- Only 15% believe the information they gather is accurate and useful

**The Insight:**
Career tools and generic chatbots don't solve this because they operate in silos. A student needs cross-domain reasoning — connecting job posts, professor research areas, hackathon deadlines, and their own academic stage into personalized, actionable guidance. No single tool does this today.

**The Solution — Elevate:**
Elevate transforms unstructured academic and career data into structured intelligence using an AI reasoning layer. It performs cross-domain reasoning across careers, research, competitions, and academic progression.

The platform uses the LIFT framework:
- **L**ocate the right information — Research opportunities and preparation paths all in one place
- **I**nterpret what applies to you — Tailored guidance based on your interests, stage, and goals
- **F**ollow through with structure — Preparation roadmaps, reminders, and clear application steps
- **T**ime actions correctly — Opportunities and deadlines surfaced when they matter most

It ingests signals from: Job Posts, Professor Pages, Research Papers, Hackathons, Competitions, and Hiring Updates. It outputs student-aligned guidance: What to pursue, When to act, How to prepare, How to apply.

**What Elevate Provides:**
1. **Unified, Tailored Guidance** — Brings career and research information in one place, translates it to clear and personalized next steps
2. **Opportunity Awareness** — Tracks hackathons, contests, and other opportunities, notifies relevant opportunities at the right time
3. **Research Engagement** — Helps students discover professors, understand their research, and apply to open internships. Also helps professors reach a broader pool of interested students

**The Prototype:**
- Full-stack mobile and web application
- Integrated LLM Chatbot for conversational guidance
- Multiplatform Notification system
- Professor information system

**Validation:**
- Over 87% of survey respondents believe a platform like Elevate would be very useful for their academic, career, and research endeavors
- Many respondents expressed the need for a simpler, more structured way to connect with professors for projects and internships

**The Ask:**
Join the presentation to learn more. Let's reduce friction in student career and research journeys.

**Audience:** College presentation / demo day, professors and students, 5-8 minute slot.
