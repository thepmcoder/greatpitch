# Test Scenario P01: HealthTrack

## Product Brief

**Company:** HealthTrack (Series A, $4M raised)
**Product:** AI-powered wearable for elderly fall detection and health monitoring
**Stage:** Post-pilot, 500 users in 3 assisted living facilities

**The Problem:**
Falls are the #1 cause of injury death for adults 65+. 36 million falls per year in the US. Current solutions (Life Alert pendants) require the user to press a button — but 47% of falls result in the person being unable to reach or activate their device. Family members live in constant anxiety.

**The Solution:**
A lightweight wristband with accelerometer, gyroscope, and heart rate sensors. On-device AI (no cloud dependency) detects falls within 2 seconds, automatically calls emergency contacts, and shares GPS location. Also monitors daily activity patterns — a 30% decline in movement over 2 weeks predicts hospitalization with 78% accuracy.

**Traction:**
- 500 pilot users across 3 facilities
- 23 confirmed fall detections, 21 accurate (91% precision)
- 2 false positives (both during aggressive physical therapy)
- 0 missed falls
- Family NPS: +72
- Average response time reduced from 14 minutes (Life Alert industry avg) to 47 seconds

**The Ask:**
$12M Series B to expand to 50 facilities and launch direct-to-consumer channel.

**Audience:** Healthcare-focused VCs, 15-minute presentation slot.
