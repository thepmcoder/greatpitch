# Test Scenario P05: GreenFleet

## Product Brief

**Company:** GreenFleet (Series A, $8M raised)
**Product:** EV fleet management platform for last-mile delivery companies
**Stage:** 12 fleet customers, managing 2,400 vehicles

**The Problem:**
Delivery companies know EVs save money long-term (60% lower fuel costs, 40% lower maintenance). But managing an EV fleet is operationally harder than diesel. Range anxiety is real — a diesel truck doesn't care about temperature, but an EV loses 30% range in cold weather. Charging infrastructure is fragmented. Route planning must account for battery state, charging station availability, and delivery windows simultaneously. Most fleet managers still use spreadsheets and driver gut feel.

**The Solution:**
A platform that optimizes the entire EV fleet operation:
1. **Smart routing** — factors in real-time battery state, weather impact on range, charging station availability, and delivery time windows. Routes update dynamically as conditions change.
2. **Charging orchestration** — schedules overnight depot charging to minimize electricity costs (off-peak rates), manages mid-route charging stops, and prevents grid overload at depots.
3. **TCO dashboard** — real-time total cost of ownership comparison: EV vs diesel, per vehicle, per route, per driver. Makes the financial case visible daily, not just in annual reports.
4. **Predictive maintenance** — monitors battery health degradation, predicts when a vehicle needs service before it fails on route.

**Traction:**
- 12 fleet customers, 2,400 vehicles managed
- Average fleet fuel cost reduction: 58% (vs diesel equivalent)
- Unplanned vehicle downtime: reduced 72%
- Route efficiency improvement: 14% more deliveries per vehicle per day
- Charging cost optimization: 31% savings vs naive charging
- Customer retention: 100% (zero churn in 18 months)
- ARR: $2.8M, growing 22% QoQ

**The Ask:**
$25M Series B to expand nationally and add medium-duty truck support.

**Audience:** Climate tech / mobility VCs, 15-minute presentation.
